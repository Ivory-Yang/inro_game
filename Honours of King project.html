Basic rules

Victory Conditions

  • Destroy the enemy crystal (base), or the enemy surrender
  • Each game lasts about 15-20 minutes and is divided into three stages: the early stage of the line, the middle stage of the group battle and the late stage of the final victory.
Map Construction
  • Combat path (Warrior/tank), middle path (Mage), development path (Archer assistance), play wild position wandering support.
  • Distribution Red and blue BUFF Tyrants (early resources) dominate (mid-late advance advantages) and other key wild monsters.
